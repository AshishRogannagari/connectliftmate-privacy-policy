ConnectLiftMate is a Chrome extension co-created by Aashish and Pavan. It is built with a modern SaaS-style experience to simplify how you manage LinkedIn connection notes, messaging templates, and Boolean search queries.

1. No Personal Data Collected
We do not collect, track, or transmit any personal or sensitive user data. Everything you save in ConnectLiftMate is stored securely inside your own browser using chrome.storage.local.

2. No External Server Communication
This extension works entirely offline. It never communicates with external servers or APIs. Your LinkedIn content remains private and controlled by you.

3. Permission Usage Transparency
- storage: To save notes, messages, and queries inside your browser.
- tabs: To identify and communicate with the active LinkedIn tab.
- host_permissions: To insert saved content into LinkedIn pages effectively.

4. Your Data Stays Yours
All your data is stored locally within your Chrome browser profile. Upon uninstalling the extension, all associated data is permanently removed.
